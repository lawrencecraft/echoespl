Weekend, February 27 & 28 , 2016-Echoes Program 1608A

Ane Brun Plays Live on Echoes.

Songs of empowerment ring out when Swedish singer Ane Brun comes in to play music from her latest album, When I’m Free. She takes her sometimes tribal songs and strips them down to just piano, guitar and voice when she makes the expansive intimate. Join John Diliberto with Ane Brun live on Echoes
